Welcome to Zap!

A soundboard for streamers and content creators.

  • Add Sound — Upload audio files (MP3, WAV, OGG, etc.)
  • Play — Click any sound card to play instantly
  • Hotkeys — Assign keyboard shortcuts to sounds
  • Groups — Organize sounds into groups
  • Collections — Create multiple soundboards

Sound Cards

Each sound has a card with controls:

  • ▶ Play — Click to play. Click again to pause (if enabled).
  • ■ Stop / ▾ Fade — Appear while playing.
  • 🔁 Loop — Toggle repeat. When on, next-sound & gap are cleared.
  • ⋯ Edit — Change name, color, hotkey, volume, and more.
  • Drag & drop — Reorder sounds by dragging cards.

Tips

  • Safety Mode — Limits to one sound at a time.
  • Export / Import — Backup your entire soundboard as a .zap file.
  • Clear Cache — Free up storage space.
  • Audio is cached — First play may have a brief delay while decoding.
